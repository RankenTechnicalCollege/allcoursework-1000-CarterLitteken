  • 2 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

Heat 2 quarts of water with 1 tablespoon salt in a large pot on high heat until boiling. Add the egg noodles. Cook for 5 minutes. Reserve 1/4 cup of the pasta cooking liquid and drain the noodles.
Place the drained hot noodles in a large bowl. Add the butter, Otp.(and Parmesan), and stir until the butter has melted. Stir in a tablespoon or two of the pasta cooking water. Otp.(Stir in the chopped parsley.) Add salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
