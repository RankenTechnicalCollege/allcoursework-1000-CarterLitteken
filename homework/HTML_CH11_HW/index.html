Books

There are three books you will need to play Dungeons and Dragons (D&D)

  • The Player's Handbook (PHB)
  • Monster Manual (MM)
  • Dungeon Master's Guild (DMG)

Make sure the books are all from the same edition.

Tools

There are many tools used to play D&D but there are 2 that are always needed.

  • Polyhedral Dice Set (7-Die Set)
  • Pencil and Eraser

DO NOT USE A PEN

Roles

You need to decide who is going to be the Dungeon Master (DM) and who are the players.

The DM is the one who creates and or chooses the adventure and encounters that the players explore and fight in.

The players make a character, that they will then play as in order to explore the adventure and or story that the DM chooses or creates
