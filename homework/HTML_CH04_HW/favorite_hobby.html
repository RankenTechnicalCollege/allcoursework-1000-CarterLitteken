Writing

Writing is a hobby I have got into more recently. I started a little over a year ago. I don't write in a traditional sense of sitting down and coming up with everything on my own.
I get help from AI to help generate parts of the story, and most of the time it's just characters and their personalities. I also use characters or settings that other people came up with.
They allow people that struggle with character creation and world building to use them for their own personal writing.
