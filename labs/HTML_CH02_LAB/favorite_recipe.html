Buttered Noodles

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook until their ready.
  2. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  3. Add the unsalted butter to the hot pasta and stir until the butter is completely melted and the pasta is evenly coated.
  4. Season with salt to taste.
  5. Serve immediately.
