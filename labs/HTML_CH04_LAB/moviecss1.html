The Iron Giant

The Iron Giant is a 1999 American animated science fiction film produced by Warner Bros. Feature Animation and directed by Brad Bird. The film is based on the 1968 novel "The Iron Man" by Ted Hughes and stars the voices of Eli Marienthal, Jennifer Aniston, Harry Connick Jr., Vin Diesel, and others.
